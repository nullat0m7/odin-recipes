Hamburger

Description

This burger is very juicy, packed with many different rich flavors and no crumb buns. Add condiments and toppings to your liking. Perfect for grilling.

Ingredients

  • Beef (1.5 pounds to start)
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Egg (for moisture and texture)
  • Seasoning (for extra flavor)
  • Condiments
  • Buns (preferably toasted)

Steps

  1. Add the ingredients to a bowl
  2. Combine the ingredients together
  3. Use the mixture to make 4 patties
  4. Cook the patties on a grill until no longer pink inside
  5. Assemble burger and enjoy!
