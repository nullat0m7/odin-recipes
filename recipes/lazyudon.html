Lazy Udon

Description

This lazy udon recipe is a tasty noodle dish you can make in 5 minutes! You will most certainly love this dish once you've tried it!

Ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Udon noodles
  • Mushrooms
  • Onion
  • Sesame oil
  • Gochujang
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the udon noodles in accordance with package
  2. Heat a (non-stick) pan on high heat
  3. Add a small amount of oil and tofu into the pan
  4. Mix together the ingredients to make the sauce
  5. After tofu is cooked, remove it and saut√© mushrooms
  6. Drain the udon and put it into the pan
  7. Add tofu back into the pan
  8. Stir in with sesame oil, and turn off the stove
  9. Enjoy!
